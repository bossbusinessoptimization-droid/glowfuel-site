Introduction

GlowFuel ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit glowfuel.com.

Information We Collect

  • Email address: When you subscribe to our newsletter or download our free tracker, collected through our email service provider (Kit/ConvertKit)

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are located in the European Union or California, you have specific rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to request correction or deletion of your data
  • The right to unsubscribe from our emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@bossbusinesspro.com.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data: Amazon Associates, Google Analytics, Kit (email), Payhip (payments), and Pinterest. Each has its own privacy policy governing their use of your information.

Data Security
