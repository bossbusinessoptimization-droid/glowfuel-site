Day 1 · Glute Training

Why Your Glutes Aren't Growing (And How to Fix It)

If you've been squatting for months and still not seeing the glute results you want, you're not alone — and it's probably not your workout. It's this.

The Activation Problem

The #1 reason women don't see glute growth isn't lack of effort — it's that their glutes simply aren't firing during exercises. Your body has learned to compensate, using your quads and lower back instead of your glutes. This is called gluteal amnesia, and it's extremely common.

The fix? Activation exercises before every workout. Specifically: resistance band work that isolates the glute medius and maximus before loading them.

The GlowFuel Activation Protocol (Do This First)

  1. Clamshells — 15 reps each side, band just above the knees
  2. Lateral band walks — 20 steps each direction
  3. Glute bridges with 2-second squeeze — 15 reps, focus on the squeeze at the top

Do these three movements before every GlowFuel workout this week. You'll feel the difference within 2 sessions.

Day 3 · Waist Training

The 5-Minute Morning Waist Routine That Actually Works

You don't need an hour in the gym to work your waist. These five minutes, done consistently every morning, build the foundation the rest of the GlowFuel plan builds on.

Why Morning Is the Best Time

Your core is "fresh" in the morning — no fatigue from the day's movement. That means you can recruit more muscle fibers with less effort. Five focused minutes beats thirty distracted minutes every time.

The 5-Minute Morning Sequence

  1. Standing oblique crunches — 30 seconds each side
  2. Dead bug — 45 seconds
  3. Side plank hold — 20 seconds each side
  4. Bicycle crunches — 45 seconds slow and controlled
  5. Deep belly breathing — 60 seconds (activates the transverse abdominis)
