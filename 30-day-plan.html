The GlowFuel
30-Day Plan

Four progressive weeks of targeted workouts and a meal plan built specifically for your waist and glutes. No gym. No guesswork.

How this works: Each week builds on the last. Do the workouts 4–5 days per week, follow the meal focus, and track your progress. Most women start feeling stronger by Day 7 and seeing visible changes by Day 21. Take measurements on Day 1, 7, 14, 21, and 30. Let's glow.
Week 1 · Days 1–7
Activate

This week is about waking up muscles that have been dormant. You're building the neuromuscular connection that makes every following week more effective. Don't rush — focus on feeling the squeeze.

The Workout (4–5x this week)
  • 1
    Glute Bridges — 3 sets × 15 reps, 2-second squeeze at top
  • 2
    Romanian Deadlifts — 3 sets × 12 reps (water bottles or light weights)
  • 3
    Dead Bug Core Holds — 3 sets × 10 reps, 8 seconds each
  • 4
    Clamshells — 15 each side with resistance band above knees
  • 5
    Lateral Band Walks — 20 steps each direction

🍽️ Meal Focus: High Protein, Reduce Sugar

Aim for 0.8g of protein per pound of body weight. Build meals around chicken, eggs, Greek yogurt, and fish. Cut refined sugar and alcohol this week. Breakfast example: protein smoothie with berries and almond butter.

Week 2 · Days 8–14
Build

Now we increase resistance and range of motion. Your glutes are activated — time to load them. Add weight or band resistance where you can.

The Workout (4–5x this week)
  • 1
    Hip Thrusts — 4 sets × 12 reps, full extension
  • 2
    Sumo Squats — 3 sets × 15 reps, deep range
  • 3
    Bicycle Crunches — 3 sets × 20 reps, slow and controlled
  • 4
    Single-Leg Glute Bridges — 12 each side
  • 5
    Side Plank Hip Dips — 12 each side

🍽️ Meal Focus: Complex Carbs Post-Workout

Increase protein to 1g per pound. Add complex carbs after workouts — sweet potato, brown rice, oats — to fuel muscle growth. This is when your glutes grow, so don't under-eat.

Week 3 · Days 15–21
Burn

Intensity rises with plyometric and circuit training. This week accelerates fat burn around the waist while continuing to build the glutes.

The Workout (4–5x this week)
  • 1
    Jump Squats — 4 sets × 15 reps, explosive
  • 2
    Donkey Kicks — 3 sets × 20 reps each side
  • 3
    Russian Twists — 3 sets × 25 reps
  • 4
    Curtsy Lunges — 12 each side
  • 5
    Ab Roller Rollouts — 3 sets × 10 reps

🍽️ Meal Focus: Anti-Inflammatory, 8-Hour Window

Try an 8-hour eating window (e.g. 11am–7pm). Focus on anti-inflammatory foods: berries, leafy greens, turmeric, ginger, salmon. This reduces bloating and reveals waist definition.

Week 4 · Days 22–30
Glow

The finale. Full circuit training with progressive overload. Your body is ready to push further than Day 1 you could have imagined.

The Workout (5x this week)
  • 1
    Full Circuit × 4 rounds — combine all previous movements
  • 2
    Hip Thrusts — 4 sets × 15 reps, heavier resistance
  • 3
    Jump Squats — 4 sets × 20 reps
  • 4
    Full Plank Series — 60 seconds each variation
  • 5
    Progressive overload — increase weight/reps from Week 2
